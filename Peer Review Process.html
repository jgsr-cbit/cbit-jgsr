Peer Review Process

The Journal of Gateway to Sustainable Research follows a rigorous, transparent, and ethical peer review process to ensure the publication of high-quality and credible research in the field of sustainable science and technology.

1. Manuscript Submission

Authors must submit their manuscripts through the journal’s official online submission system.

Submission must include:

  • Complete manuscript (as per Author Guidelines)
  • Cover letter
  • Ethical approval statement (if applicable)
  • Conflict of interest declaration
  • Supplementary files (data, figures, permissions, etc., if required)

Authors are responsible for ensuring originality and adherence to publication ethics before submission.

2. Initial Editorial Screening (Technical & Scope Check)

Technical Review:

  • Formatting compliance
  • Adherence to journal guidelines
  • Plagiarism screening
  • Completeness of submission documents

Editorial Evaluation:

  • Relevance to the journal’s scope
  • Scientific quality and originality
  • Language clarity and presentation
  • Ethical compliance

Possible Outcomes:

  • Desk Rejection
  • Proceed to Peer Review

3. Reviewer Assignment and Invitation

The Academic Editor selects 2–3 independent expert reviewers based on:

  • Research experience and publication record
  • Subject expertise
  • Absence of conflicts of interest

4. Peer Review Evaluation

  • Originality and Novelty – Contribution of new knowledge
  • Scientific Soundness – Methodology, analysis, and data reliability
  • Significance – Impact and relevance to sustainable research
  • Clarity and Structure – Organization and readability
  • Ethical Compliance – Adherence to ethical standards

Review Model: Double-Blind Peer Review System.

5. Editorial Decision

  • Accept (Rare)
  • Minor Revisions
  • Major Revisions
  • Reject

6. Author Revision and Resubmission

  • Submit a revised manuscript
  • Provide a detailed point-by-point response
  • Highlight all changes made

7. Re-review and Final Decision

The revised manuscript may be sent back to reviewers or evaluated directly by the Editor. The Editor’s decision is final.

8. Production and Publication

  • Copyediting
  • Typesetting
  • Author proofreading
  • Final quality check

After approval, the article is published online as Open Access. The journal works toward inclusion in recognized indexing databases to ensure visibility and academic recognition.
