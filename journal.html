About CBIT

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), Proddatur, established in 2008 under Bharathi Educational Society, is a premier institution approved by AICTE and affiliated to JNTUA. Known for academic excellence, advanced infrastructure, and strong focus on research and innovation, CBIT fosters industry-ready technocrats and entrepreneurs. The institute was honored with the Best Innovation-Driven Organization Award at NICE-TEAS Asia 2025 (Vietnam) and achieved national recognition with its Stratox 2025 helium balloon satellite, recorded in the Indian Book of World Records.

About JGSR

The Journal of Gateway to Sustainable Research is a fully Open Access journal dedicated to advancing high-quality scientific research and knowledge in the field of sustainable development. The journal ensures that all published articles are freely and immediately accessible online without subscription or paywall barriers, promoting global knowledge sharing among researchers, policymakers, industry professionals, and the public. Authors retain full copyright of their work, and all articles are published under the Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY) License, allowing free sharing and adaptation with proper attribution. Following the Gold Open Access model, the journal maintains transparent and affordable publication practices with a nominal Article Processing Charge (APC) payable only after acceptance. The journal is committed to long-term digital preservation and ethical publishing standards, ensuring permanent accessibility and wide dissemination of published research.
