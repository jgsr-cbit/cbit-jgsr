Author Guidelines

The Journal of Gateway to Sustainable Research (JGSR) publishes high-quality, peer-reviewed research contributing to environmental, technological, industrial, and societal sustainability. Authors must carefully follow the instructions below before submission.

1. Submission & Ethical Requirements

Originality

  • The manuscript must be original and unpublished.
  • It must not be under review in any other journal.

Plagiarism Policy

  • Similarity index must be below 20% (excluding references).
  • Proper citation and referencing are mandatory.
  • Self-plagiarism is not permitted.
  • AI-generated plagiarism or unethical content is strictly prohibited.
  • All manuscripts will undergo plagiarism screening.

Authorship

  • Maximum number of authors: 6
  • All listed authors must have made significant academic contributions.
  • The corresponding author must ensure approval from all co-authors before submission.

Ethical Compliance

For research involving:

  • Human subjects
  • Animal experiments
  • Hazardous materials

Authors must provide relevant ethical approval details.

Generative AI

  • Use of Generative AI tools must be disclosed.
  • AI tools cannot be listed as authors.
  • Fabricated data, references, or manipulated content is strictly prohibited.
  • Authors remain fully responsible for all submitted content.

Copyright

Authors may be required to sign a publication agreement upon acceptance.
